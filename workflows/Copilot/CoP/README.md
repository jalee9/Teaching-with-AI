# Community of Practice (CoP) Workflows

These workflows were developed as part of a Community of Practice (CoP) at Wake Tech focused on integrating Microsoft Copilot into teaching and academic workflows.

## Purpose

- Share practical strategies among faculty  
- Explore real classroom use cases  
- Iterate and refine workflows over time  

## Note

These workflows may be more contextual and evolving than general workflows. They reflect active experimentation and collaboration.

## Available Workflows

- Grading Feedback  
- Assignment Design  
- Student Support
